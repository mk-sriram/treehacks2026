Manufacturing

Our Capabilities

01

Wafer Fabrication

Class 100 / ISO 5 cleanroom. 200mm and 300mm lines. CMOS, BCD, SOI, and MEMS process platforms from 350nm to 90nm.

02

Wafer Thinning & Dicing

Back-grinding to 50μm. Blade and laser dicing. Stealth dicing for ultra-thin die. Tape-and-reel packaging.

03

Advanced Packaging

Flip-chip, fan-out WLP, 2.5D interposer. Cu pillar and micro-bump technology. Known-good-die testing.

Testing & Reliability

Wafer probe, final test, burn-in. HTOL, TC, HAST, ESD per JEDEC standards. Failure analysis lab on-site.

05

Material Science Lab

SEM, TEM, XRD, SIMS characterization. Process development and optimization. Thin film and etch R&D.

06

Global Logistics

Direct shipping to 40+ countries. Bonded warehouse in Hsinchu and Kaohsiung. DDP / FOB / CIF terms available.
